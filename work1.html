Chalo

Chalo is India #1 bus transport technology company that provides live bus bus tracking services and contactless payment solutions to transform everyday bus travel into a safer and more reliable experience.


About The Project

A project developed developed to broadcast the innovations of Chalo towards simplifying commute. The project was to develop a website where a user can get all the information about the company, and also know all the current innovations and solutions offered by Chalo. UI/UX was integral in this application to communicate to users from all walks of the society.

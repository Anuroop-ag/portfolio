Chalo

Chalo is India #1 bus transport technology company that provides live bus bus tracking services and contactless payment solutions to transform everyday bus travel into a safer and more reliable experience.


About The Project

A project developed developed to broadcast the innovations of Chalo towards simplifying commute. The project was to develop a website where a user can get all the information about the company, and also know all the current innovations and solutions offered by Chalo. UI/UX was integral in this application to communicate to users from all walks of the society.

Key Objectives and Responsibilities

  • Orchestrated Contactless Payment Solutions ( Chalo Card ) to increase user experience by more than 50% and get approved in 20+ cities owing to it’s reception.
  • Developed online onboarding process for eKYC within 2 seconds and thereby, increasing "Net Promoter Score ( NPS )" by 60%. Tracked user experiences to showcase a witness over 75% users to shift to cashless methods with this eKYC process
  • Designed "Automated Fare Collection System ( AFCS )" for Conductorless buses in India leading to addition of 30+ citieswith its success into the company’s operations.
  • Designed and developed systems to handle over millions of online transaction accounts for seamless travel.
  • Increased security of the existing systems by introducing Central Authorization System

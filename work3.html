Leave Management System, Wipro

Developed Leave Management System for Wipro to simplify leaves/ holiday process.. At this project, I acted as a Front End/Angular Developer.


About The Project

A project developed as an intern under Wipro. The project was to develop an internal tool to manage leaves of the employees. At this project, I acted as a Web Developer and built all the games using Angular. The main challenge was to ship the product in the given time with added features.

Key Objectives and Responsibilities

  • Designed and developed the "Leave Management System" to manage leaves in hierarchical organisations with employees greater than 100,000. Seamlessly integrated with foolproof and cyber-secure solutions.
